Our Story

The Journey of VelvetFork

Our dining room at twilight
At VelvetFork, we believe dining is more than sustenance—it's a story shared between people, a moment of connection, a memory being made.

Established in 2015, VelvetFork began as a simple vision: to create a space where exceptional food meets genuine hospitality. What started as a small neighborhood restaurant has grown into a destination for those who appreciate the art of fine dining, yet never want to sacrifice comfort for elegance.

Our philosophy is rooted in respect—for ingredients, for tradition, and for the craft of cooking. We work closely with local farmers and fishermen to source the finest seasonal produce, ensuring each dish tells the story of its origin while honoring the techniques passed down through generations.

Every evening at VelvetFork is a carefully orchestrated experience. From the warm greeting at the door to the final farewell, we strive to create an atmosphere where guests feel both celebrated and at ease. Our team approaches service not as a transaction, but as an opportunity to create something meaningful.

Seasonal & Thoughtful

We craft our menu around what's fresh and at its peak, working directly with trusted local producers to ensure quality from farm to table.

Transparent Kitchen

Our open kitchen design invites you to witness the care and precision behind each dish, creating trust through visibility.

Attentive Service

Our staff is trained to anticipate needs without intrusion, creating a seamless experience that feels both personal and polished.

Our Approach

We believe the best dining experiences happen when traditional wisdom meets modern sensibility, when comfort meets refinement, and when exceptional ingredients meet skilled hands.

Great food should feel like coming home to a place you've never been before—familiar in its warmth, surprising in its beauty, and memorable in its execution.

Our Journey

Key moments that have shaped the VelvetFork experience

2015
VelvetFork opens its doors in a historic building with just 12 tables. Chef Elena Rossi introduces her vision of modern comfort cuisine.
2017
We establish direct partnerships with local organic farms, ensuring traceability and peak freshness for every ingredient.
2019
Introduction of the Chef's Table experience, offering an intimate view into our kitchen and a personalized tasting menu.
2021
Complete renovation of our dining space, expanding capacity while maintaining the intimate atmosphere that defines us.
2023
Launch of our sustainability initiative, committing to zero food waste and carbon-neutral kitchen operations.

Our Commitment

Creating exceptional dining experiences while caring for our community and environment

At VelvetFork, we believe exceptional dining must go hand-in-hand with responsible practices. Our commitment to sustainability extends from our sourcing to our service.

We work exclusively with local farmers and producers who share our values, ensuring our ingredients travel minimal distances to reach your plate. Our kitchen operates on a zero-waste philosophy, finding creative uses for every part of our ingredients.

Our dining room features furniture crafted from reclaimed materials, energy-efficient lighting, and a comprehensive recycling program. We believe that luxury and sustainability are not opposing concepts, but complementary ideals that together create a truly exceptional experience.

Looking forward, we're committed to becoming a carbon-neutral establishment by 2025, continuing our journey of thoughtful, responsible hospitality.
