A Culinary Experience Like No Other

Nestled in the heart of the city, Velvet Fork offers an unforgettable dining experience that combines modern culinary techniques with time-honored traditions. Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, respect the craft, and create dishes that tell a story.

Our chefs draw inspiration from global cuisines while staying true to our commitment to seasonal, locally-sourced produce. Every plate is a work of art, designed to delight both the palate and the eye.

At Velvet Fork, we believe dining is not just about nourishment, but about creating memories. From the moment you enter our doors, you'll be immersed in an atmosphere of warmth, elegance, and exceptional service.

Signature Creations

Truffle-Infused Wild Mushroom Risotto

Carnaroli rice slowly cooked with wild foraged mushrooms, finished with white truffle oil and Parmigiano-Reggiano. A symphony of earthy flavors that embodies the essence of Italian culinary tradition with a modern twist.

Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb

New Zealand lamb encrusted with rosemary, thyme, and Dijon mustard, served with roasted baby vegetables and a red wine reduction. Tender, flavorful, and expertly prepared to highlight the natural qualities of premium lamb.

Chocolate Soufflé with Gold Leaf

An airy dark chocolate soufflé with a molten center, dusted with edible gold leaf and served with a side of vanilla bean ice cream. The perfect balance of richness and lightness to conclude your dining experience.

An Atmosphere of Refined Comfort

Velvet Fork's interior was designed by award-winning architect Elena Rossi, who envisioned a space that balances contemporary elegance with timeless warmth. The dining room features custom-made furniture, ambient lighting, and curated art pieces that create an intimate yet sophisticated atmosphere.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our menu to our restaurant's design. We use reclaimed wood, energy-efficient lighting, and locally-crafted decor elements to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing aesthetic appeal.

Dining at Velvet Fork is not merely a meal—it's a journey through flavors, textures, and emotions that will linger in your memory long after the last bite.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, conducting a business dinner, or simply enjoying a night out, our staff is dedicated to providing impeccable service that anticipates your needs without intruding on your experience.
